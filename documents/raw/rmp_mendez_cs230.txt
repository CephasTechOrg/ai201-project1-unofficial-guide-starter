CS 230 - Web Development   |   Quality: 5.0   Difficulty: 3.0   |   Apr 2024
Mendez's class is the most practical one I've taken. You build a full-stack app over the semester — React front end, Node/Express API, a real database. Grading is project-based, almost no exams, and her rubrics are crystal clear. She gives feedback like a code reviewer at a real company. Take it before you start applying for internships.
