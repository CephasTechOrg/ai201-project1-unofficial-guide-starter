CS 210 - Discrete Mathematics   |   Quality: 5.0   Difficulty: 3.5   |   Apr 2024
Bello explains proofs more clearly than any math professor I've had. Induction finally clicked for me in her class. The catch is the homework volume — weekly problem sets that take 4-6 hours. But every problem prepares you for the exams, which are fair. Do NOT take this class if you plan to skip the homework; it's 30% of the grade and the practice IS the learning.
