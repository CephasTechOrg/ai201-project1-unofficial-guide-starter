CS 350 - Software Engineering   |   Quality: 4.0   Difficulty: 3.0   |   Apr 2024
The content is genuinely useful — Agile, version control, testing, code review, working in a team. The problem is the entire grade rides on one semester-long group project, and if your team is bad, you're stuck. Halvorsen is hands-off, which is realistic but frustrating when teams fall apart. Pick your group carefully if you can.
