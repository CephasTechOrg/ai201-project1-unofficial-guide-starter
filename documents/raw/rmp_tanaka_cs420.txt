CS 420 - Machine Learning   |   Quality: 5.0   Difficulty: 5.0   |   Apr 2024
Tanaka is demanding and research-heavy, but if you keep up it's the best class in the department. You implement everything from scratch in NumPy — linear regression, backprop, a small CNN — before you're allowed to touch PyTorch. The math prereqs are real; if your linear algebra is rusty, review it first. Office hours feel like grad seminars.

Hardest workload I've had but I came out able to actually read ML papers. Weekly problem sets mix proofs and coding. The final is a research-style project you present to the class. Tanaka holds you to a high standard and the feedback is blunt but useful. Not a class to take in a heavy semester.
