CS 250 - Computer Architecture   |   Quality: 4.0   Difficulty: 3.5   |   Apr 2024
Reed is dry but fair, and he's organized in a way Patel never is. Lectures are dense — pipelining, caches, assembly — but he posts clear notes. Heads up: attendance is graded with random in-class clicker questions, so you can't skip. Exams are predictable if you do the practice problems. Solid, if unexciting.
