CS 101 - Intro to Programming   |   Quality: 5.0   Difficulty: 2.0   |   Apr 2024
If you have never written a line of code in your life, take CS 101 with Okonkwo. He genuinely slows down for beginners and never makes you feel dumb for asking a basic question. The pace is gentle and the assignments build on each other. Grading is transparent — rubrics are posted before every assignment. Best first CS class you could ask for.
