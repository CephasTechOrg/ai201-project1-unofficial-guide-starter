CS 340 - Operating Systems   |   Quality: 5.0   Difficulty: 5.0   |   Apr 2024
The workload is insane but I learned more in OS than in any other class. The projects are in C — you build a shell, a memory allocator, and a tiny scheduler. Each one took me 20+ hours. Lindqvist is an amazing lecturer though, very clear, and the projects are what make the class worth it. Start them the day they're assigned, not the weekend before.
